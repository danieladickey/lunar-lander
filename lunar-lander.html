Instructions

You have one job, to land the lunar lander safely. To do so, you must be moving at two m/s or less and be at an upright angle between 5 and 355 degrees. You only have 20 seconds worth of fuel. Use it wisely.

You will have three seconds to survey the surface and locate one of two landing zones before your craft begins plummeting to the surface. After successfully landing on the surface, you will have three seconds to prepare to land one more time at a new location, this time with only one safe landing zone. Good luck!
